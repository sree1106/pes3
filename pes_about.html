Mission and Vision Mission and Vision
Mission

To be the leading provider of scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and the preferred professional development source for our members.

Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in our industry, setting benchmarks for success.

Our Objectives

Promoting Knowledge and Innovation

The PES encourages and supports research, development, and application of technologies and solutions to meet global energy challenges.

Disseminating Technical Information

The society provides forums, publications, and events to share knowledge on topics like power systems, renewable energy, energy storage, and grid modernization.

Fostering Professional Development

IEEE PES offers opportunities for education, training, and networking to help professionals stay up to date with industry developments and grow their careers.

Global Collaboration

The society aims to create international partnerships and collaborations to address energy challenges on a global scale, encouraging the exchange of ideas and solutions.

Advocacy and Standards Development

The PES works on developing industry standards and policies that address technical, economic, and environmental challenges in the power and energy sectors.

Supporting Sustainability

IEEE PES is focused on promoting sustainability through the integration of renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, and environmental considerations in power systems.
